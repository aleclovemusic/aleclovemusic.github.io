Biography

.

A composer for a wide range of bands and ensembles, Alec David Love Kunkel started his career composing marching band music for his hometown high school in Alma, Michigan. The marching band played many of his arrangements and original works starting as a high school junior. After reveiving his diploma in 2018 he attended Lansing Community College, and planned on transferring to Michigan State University for an Astrophysics major. He occasionally taught marching percussion at summer band camps while developing his composing expertise on the side.

During the coronavirus pandemic in 2020 he explored more avenues of composition. He entered in numerous film scoring competitions in 2021 and 2022, earning honorable mentions in two competitions, and began composing A Day in Eternity, his first orchestral suite.
